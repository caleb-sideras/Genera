Q1 2022 Road Map

Creator Fund

A portion of capital allocation will go towards the creator fund. This fund will support artists from a multitude of backgrounds in their pursuit of creating NFTs. This fund will help with minting, generation and marketing costs. Artists chosen for this fund will be determined based on community voting and internal feedback.

NFT minter/Texture mapping

Both these features are available in our 'NFT Maker' section. But through community feedback, we have learned that artists also want them as stand alone features.

Solana and Polygon Minting

We intend to add functionality that will enable users to mint their NFT collections to more blockchains. Solana & Polygon support was chosen first.

Public Minting

As we develop our community features (public user profiles, profile/collection searches, public collection showcases etc) we will allow Genera users to set up public minted collections. Where any user could preview a collection and mint an NFT from that collection.

Misc

More complex rarity control, gif/video support, collection sorting, UI improvements and more!
